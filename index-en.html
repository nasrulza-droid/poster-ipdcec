What You Need to Prepare

Poster Format

  • Digital poster, A3 size (297 x 420 mm), portrait orientation
  • File format PDF or JPG/JPEG (max. 10 MB)
  • Minimum resolution 300 dpi
  • Language: Indonesian or English

Required Content

  • Title, issue/problem, proposed solution, expected impact
  • IPDCEC logo + school logo in the top-right corner
  • References (if needed)
  • AI disclosure if AI tools are used

Registration Documents

  • Student ID card / active enrollment letter
  • Proof of following @ipdcec2026
  • Proof of uploading twibbon and promo poster
  • Final poster upload based on requirements
